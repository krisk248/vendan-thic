The Snake and the Rope

In darkness, one mistakes a rope for a snake and becomes frightened. Upon bringing light, the snake disappears and only the rope remains. Similarly, through ignorance (avidya), we superimpose the world upon Brahman and become bound by fear and desire. When the light of knowledge dawns, the world as separate from Brahman disappears, and only the one Reality remains.
